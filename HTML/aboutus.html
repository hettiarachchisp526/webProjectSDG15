About Us

  Who We Are ?

Greenland is an organization that conserves mountains, wetlands, drylands and freshwater, and works to stop uncontrolled deforestation and to stand up against desertification by 2030 and to protect endangered species as well as endangered species.

  Our Mission

The mission of our Green Land website is to protect and conserve all ecosystems, to rehabilitate various ecologically affected lands, to protect endangered species, and to take the necessary steps to reduce desertification by 2030.

  Social Responsibility

It is very important to abide by the law of the land, which is the primary responsibility of the society. All measures required to protect the animals and the plants of the country while protecting the environmental laws enacted by the government should be implemented within the social spheres from the home to the government.

  Our Vision

Our vision is to address the various environmental issues that currently affect both species of animals and plants in the country through the social awareness provided through this website in the near future.

  Precision

We work carefully and well. Because we want to minimize all threats to the terrestrial life of the land as much as we can to keep the site running smoothly.

  Performance

Our performance is above average. We are trying to achieve all the targets of life on land.

  Commitment

We dedicate ourselves with zeal. We stand behind our work and deliver on our promises.
