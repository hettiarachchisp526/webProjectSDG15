    DID YOU KNOW ?

    7500

    About 7 500 plant species constitute the flora of Sri Lanka. Flowering plants constituted about 3 360 species, belonged to 1 350 genera and 200 families. The flora contains by about 830 endemic species.

    361

    According to environmental groups, 361 elephants perished in Sri Lanka in 2019, a new high.According to environmentalists, this is the biggest number of elephant fatalities observed since Sri Lanka gained independence in 1948

    164,942

    srilanka has lost 9,497 hectares primary forests since 2002 to 2018 and 164,942 hectares tree cover lost since 2001 to 2018

    830

    There are 830 indigenous species in Sri Lanka, with 217 trees and woody climbers found in the lowland wet zone. There are 139 (64%) of these species in the reserve, including 16 uncommon species. Birds have the highest level of endemism, with 19 (95%) of the 20 species reported in the property being indigenous to Sri Lanka. Endemism is also more than 50% among mammals and butterflies.
